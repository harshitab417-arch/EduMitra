# EduMitra Pitch — Winning Narrative

## Core Insight Most Teams Will Miss
Most teams will present **features**:
- progress tracking
- mentor matching
- dashboards

But judges don’t reward feature lists.

### Our Theory of Change
NGO tutoring programmes don’t fail due to:
- lack of content
- lack of intent

They fail due to **lack of systems**.

**EduMitra is not an ed-tech app.**  
It is an **operations layer** that makes human teaching:
- measurable
- trackable
- reproducible

---

## 1. Open With a Story (Not a Statistic)

**Ananya’s Story (45 sec)**

> Ananya is 11. She’s in Grade 5 in a government school in Hyderabad.  
> She hasn’t understood fractions since Grade 3.  
>  
> Her volunteer tutor — a well-meaning engineering student — has met her six times this year.  
> Each session, he teaches something different. No plan.  
>  
> No one knows Ananya is falling behind.  
> By Grade 7, she’ll be two years behind in Maths.  
>  
> No one will notice until it’s too late.

**Transition:**
> Now let’s see what changes when her NGO uses EduMitra.
